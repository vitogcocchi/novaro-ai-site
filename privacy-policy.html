3. SMS Communications

By opting in to receive SMS messages from Novaro AI, you consent to receive text messages related to our services, including business updates, appointment reminders, and marketing communications sent to the phone number you provided.

Opt-out: You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive one confirmation message and no further messages. To re-subscribe, reply START.

Help: For help, reply HELP to any SMS message or contact us at vito@novaroai.net.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.
