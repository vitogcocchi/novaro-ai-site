SMS Communications & Opt-In

How we use SMS: Novaro AI sends SMS messages to prospective and current business clients who have explicitly opted in to receive communications from us. Messages include business service information, appointment reminders, follow-up messages, and marketing communications.

Recipients opt in to receive SMS messages from Novaro AI through one of the following methods:

  • Web form opt-in: Prospective clients submit their contact information via a web form and check a box agreeing to receive SMS communications from Novaro AI.
  • Verbal opt-in: During a phone or video call, the prospect verbally consents to receive follow-up text messages.
  • Direct reply: Prospects who initiate contact and request follow-up via text message.

All SMS messages include opt-out instructions. Recipients can reply STOP at any time to be removed from all future SMS communications. Message and data rates may apply.

We do not send unsolicited SMS messages. All contacts in our outreach system have been sourced through legitimate business prospecting channels and have provided or implied consent through professional engagement.
